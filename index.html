Overview

The E-Commerce Price Monitor Web Scraper Framework is a complete, multi-platform price tracking tool designed for today's competitive e-commerce landscape. Built with pure Node.js and advanced scraping technology, this powerful tool automatically tracks prices across major e-commerce platforms including Amazon, eBay, Walmart, and BestBuy. Users can monitor competitor pricing in real-time, receive instant alerts for price drops, and export comprehensive data for strategic analysis‚Äîall while maintaining respectful scraping practices with built-in rate limiting.
